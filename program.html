Educational Programs

Program 1: Introduction to Computer Science

This program is designed for students who are new to computer science. It covers the basics of programming, algorithms, and data structures.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Prerequisites: None

Program 2: Advanced Computer Science

This program is designed for students who have already completed the Introduction to Computer Science program. It covers advanced topics such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, and computer networks.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Prerequisites: Introduction to Computer Science

Program 3: Introduction to Data Science

This program is designed for students who are interested in data science. It covers the basics of data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Prerequisites: None

Program 4: Advanced Data Science

This program is designed for students who have already completed the Introduction to Data Science program. It covers advanced topics such as deep learning, natural language processing, and big data analytics.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Prerequisites: Introduction to Data Science

Program 5: Introduction to Cybersecurity

This program is designed for students who are interested in cybersecurity. It covers the basics of network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Prerequisites: None

Program 6: Advanced Cybersecurity

This program is designed for students who have already completed the Introduction to Cybersecurity program. It covers advanced topics such as malware analysis, penetration testing, and incident response.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Prerequisites: Introduction to Cybersecurity
