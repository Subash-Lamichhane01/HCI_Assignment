E-Learning is an innovative educational institute established in 1985 that offers a unique and convenient approach to learning. With a focus on online education. E-Learning still provides on-site learning on our state of the art facility illustrated in the images. The main focus is still to provide the students with the opportunity to acquire knowledge and skills from anywhere in the world.


At E-Learning, students can choose from a variety of courses, all of which are designed to meet the needs of modern learners. The courses are interactive, engaging, and over a wide range of topics, from business and technology to arts and humanities.


We are also committed to research and innovation, with a strong focus on creating new knowledge and solutions to real-world problems. The institute's research centers and facilities are equipped with the latest technology and resources, providing researchers tools to conduct groundbreaking research.


The institute also places a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities, providing students with a wide range of opportunities to pursue their interests and develop their skills outside the classroom. From sports and cultural activities to community and entrepreneurship, there is something for everyone at E-Learning. The institute's student clubs and organizations provide a platform for students to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and build with their peers.
